Frequently Asked Questions

How is the value guarantee calculated?

We calculate the total value based on the current Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) of the items in your box. We guarantee the total MSRP will be 25% to 250% greater than your purchase price.

What kind of products can I expect?

Our boxes contain a wide range of premium electronics from top brands. This can include headphones, smartwatches, gaming accessories, smart home devices, high-quality chargers, and more. The value and type of items scale with the price of the box you choose.

How does the 1-in-100 Jackpot work?

For every 100 mystery boxes sold across all tiers, our system randomly designates one order as a Jackpot winner. That box will include an extra, high-value bumper gift! Winners are notified via a special golden ticket inside their box!

Can I return my Mystery Box?

Due to the nature of the mystery and the guaranteed value, all Mystery Box sales are final and cannot be returned. However, if an item is defective, it is covered by our standard warranty policy.
